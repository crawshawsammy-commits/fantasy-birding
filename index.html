How to Play

📋 Squad Rules

  • Pick exactly 11 birds before heading out
  • You only score for birds in your squad
  • Squads lock once you start a gameweek — plan ahead!
  • Build a fresh squad each gameweek
  • Mix common and rare — all-rare squads are risky

👑 Captain & Bonuses

  • Captain earns double points when spotted
  • Points = the bird's rarity rank (1 = most common, 925 = rarest)
  • First-of-season bonus: first time spotting a species this year earns double points
  • Both bonuses stack — captain + first of season = 4× base points
